Web Audio sync

Both devices play the same click-loop, each locked to the wall clock through Web Audio — sample-accurate scheduling, a clock that doesn't lie, and no seeks. Open on the laptop and the phone, press Play on both, and listen: do the downbeats land as one?

Clock offset — reconcile device clocks

This proto uses each device's system clock, so a little skew between laptop and phone is normal. Nudge one device until the two lock. (In the real app, BardicClock does this automatically and tighter — here it's manual just to prove the playback holds.)
