The War of Succession  ·  Year 20

Once the jewel of the Western Kingdoms, Kirtas lies fractured — twenty years of civil war have bled the land dry. Two claimants vie for a throne while the missing heir haunts every whispered conversation. The dead walk unchecked. And to the south, Numior sharpens its ambitions.

State of the Realm

Tyrus holds the capital and believes final victory is within reach — if only he can raise enough men. Darius and Lord Reykoldt endure in the Long Hills, fighting still in the name of a prince no one has seen in years. Tiersgard remains neutral, its merchants grown rich on the tools of war. And the dead walk where armies once marched.
