Tiersgard
Coastal Trade City · The Andan Sea

The only city that retains a semblance of its former glory. Merchants grow rich on the tools of war while agents from both sides operate in its streets. Ostensibly neutral.

The Capital
Royal Seat · Kirtas

Held by Tyrus and his remaining veteran regiments. The seat of Kirtian power for generations, now garrisoned by a king increasingly desperate for men and coin.

Yevennia
Duchy · Kirtas

Home of Lord Reykoldt, Duke of Yevennia, where Darius and the King's Council first rallied in support of the young prince. The seat of the original rebellion.

The Long Hills
Highland Region · Northern Kirtas

Where Darius and Lord Reykoldt have encamped with the remnants of their forces. Remote, defensible, and slowly becoming a legend in its own right.

The Kharak Mountains
Mountain Range · South-East Kirtas

A natural barrier between Kirtas and the kingdom of Numior. Orc and Goblin warbands pour from the passes as the border watches have collapsed.

Dangerous
Ilora
Elven Forest · North-East

The great Elven forest, whose inhabitants have withdrawn entirely from the outside world. Those who stray near the treeline seldom return.

Once a vassal state of Kirtas, now an independent kingdom growing in confidence and military ambition. King Eneos II watches the north with hungry eyes.
